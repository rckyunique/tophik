Jambo & Welcome

Known as the land that hosts the largest land mammal migration in the world, Tanzania is home to diverse and vibrant cultures, distinct landscapes, natural gems and world wonders. From the spectacular annual migration of over two million of wildebeest and zebra that takes place in the Serengeti, to the “Eden Garden of East Africa”, known as the Ngorongoro Crater, and the enchanting Mahale Mountains where wild chimpanzees freely roam, Tanzania truly marks itself as the Top 10 Safari Destination in the World.

 

Experience first-hand the majestic and untamed wilderness that run, stalk and hunt for survival. Be overwhelmed by the vivid beauty that can only be experienced as you immerse yourself in the bush experience. As you cruise freely through the endless plains, watch the sunset transform the sky into a mesmerizing orange haze, and listen to the sounds of the surrounding wilderness that lull you into a dreamlike reality—you will truly come to love this place we call home with its amazing beauty for everyone to discover, Top Hiking and Safaris team will ensure that your return home will be unforgettable experience that you will love it and last for your lifetime. Karibu Tanzania!
